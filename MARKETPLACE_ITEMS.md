## How to Use

### Purchasing Items
1. Visit the **Marketplace** screen
2. Navigate through the category tabs (Equipment, Consumables, Training, etc.)
3. Select an item to view its details
4. Click "Buy" if you have enough gold and meet fame requirements

### Using Items on Gladiators
1. Go to the **Gladiators** screen
2. Select a gladiator from your roster
3. Click the **üéí Use Item** button in the detail panel
4. Select an item from your inventory
5. Click "Use Item" to apply the effect

## Item Properties

### Unlocking Items
Some items require a minimum ludus fame to unlock:
- Basic items: Available immediately
- Advanced items: 50-200 fame required
- Elite items: 300-500 fame required

### Limited Stock
Some powerful items have limited stock:
- Champion's Panoply: 1 in stock
- Miracle Cure: 1 in stock
- Legendary Combat Scroll: 1 in stock
- Arena Lion: 1 in stock
- War Bear: 1 in stock
- Imperial Introduction: 1 in stock

Stock refreshes with the market (every 10 days or manual refresh).

### Effect Types

#### Permanent Effects
- **Stat Boosts**: Increase gladiator stats permanently
- **Fame Boosts**: Instantly increase individual or ludus fame
- **XP Grants**: Instantly grant experience points
- **Skill Points**: Grant skill points for unlocking abilities

#### Temporary Effects
- **Combat Buffs**: Active for the next fight only
- **Training Boosts**: Active for several days
- **Morale Boosts**: Immediate morale increase (temporary)

#### Instant Effects
- **Healing**: Restore HP and/or stamina immediately
- **Injury Healing**: Reduce recovery time or instantly heal injuries

## Game Mechanics Integration

### Combat System
- Combat buffs from consumables apply to the next fight
- Equipment stat boosts affect damage, defense, and accuracy
- Healing items can prepare gladiators for immediate combat

### Training System
- Training boosts increase training efficiency
- XP items accelerate leveling
- Skill point items unlock new abilities faster

### Injury System
- Pain salves reduce recovery time
- Miracle cures instantly heal injuries
- Physician services provide complete healing

### Morale System
- Luxury items boost individual or roster morale
- Higher morale improves combat performance
- Oracle's blessing provides long-term morale boost

### Fame System
- Fame-boosting items unlock higher-tier content
- Individual fame increases gladiator value
- Ludus fame unlocks better marketplace items

## Tips

1. **Stock up on healing items** before major tournaments
2. **Use training boosts** during intensive training periods
3. **Save miracle cures** for your best gladiators
4. **Buy limited stock items** as soon as you can afford them
5. **Use combat buffs strategically** for important fights
6. **Invest in equipment early** for permanent stat gains
7. **Check fame requirements** before planning purchases

## Economy Balance

- **Basic items**: 30-50 gold
- **Standard items**: 60-200 gold
- **Advanced items**: 250-400 gold
- **Elite items**: 500-800 gold
- **Legendary items**: 1500+ gold

The marketplace provides meaningful choices between immediate consumables, long-term investments (equipment), and strategic advantages (buffs and training boosts).
