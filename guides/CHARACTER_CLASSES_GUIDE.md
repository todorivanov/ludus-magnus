## ğŸ® Pro Strategies

### Balanced Fighter
- Mix your actions to build up stacks
- Use all 4 action types for max bonus

### Warrior
- Fish for crits to reset cooldowns
- Build combos for bonus damage
- Use skills aggressively

### Tank
- Defend often to trigger shield
- Let passive healing sustain you
- Outlast enemies

### Glass Cannon
- Avoid damage at all costs
- Spam skills (low mana cost)
- Deal massive damage while low HP

### Bruiser
- Constantly attack to lifesteal
- Get permanent HP boosts from kills
- Sustain through damage

### Mage
- Spam skills (high mana regen)
- Use basic attacks after skills for bonus damage
- Rare but devastating crits

### Assassin
- Make first hit count (double damage)
- Build high combos
- Hope for extra attack procs

### Berserker
- Embrace low HP for damage boost
- Convert damage taken into mana
- Never defend (penalties)

### Paladin
- Defend to heal massively
- Natural regen sustains you
- Use items for huge healing

### Necromancer
- Use skills to heal via lifesteal
- Get stronger from each kill
- High mana for constant skills
