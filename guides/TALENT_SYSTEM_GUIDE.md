# Talent System Guide

## Overview

The Talent System adds deep customization and replayability to your character. Each class has **3 unique specialization trees** with multiple talent nodes that provide stat bonuses and passive abilities.

---

## Key Features

### ‚ú® **3 Talent Trees Per Class**
- Each class has unique specializations
- Trees focus on different playstyles
- Mix and match talents across trees

### üìä **Progressive Talent Points**
- Earn **1 talent point per level** (starting at level 2)
- Level 10 = 9 talent points
- Level 20 = 19 talent points

### üéØ **Talent Dependencies**
- Some talents require prerequisites
- Higher tiers require points spent in the tree
- Strategic choices matter!

### üí™ **Two Types of Effects**
1. **Stat Modifiers** - Increase strength, health, crit, etc.
2. **Passive Abilities** - Unique combat effects

### ‚öîÔ∏è **WARRIOR**

**Focus**: Weapon mastery and devastating strikes

**Key Talents**:

**Focus**: Rage-fueled relentless attacks

**Key Talents**:
- **Building Rage** (Rank 5): +1 STR, +1% Crit per rank
- **Enrage**: Critical hits grant temporary strength boost
- **Rampage** (Rank 3): Each attack increases damage of next attack (stacking)
- **Bloodthirst** (Rank 3): Restore 5% health when dealing crits
- **Reckless Abandon** (Ultimate): +20% damage, +30% crit chance

**Best For**: Sustained DPS, self-healing

---

**Focus**: Impenetrable defense and battlefield control

**Key Talents**:
- **Thick Skin** (Rank 5): +3 Defense per rank
- **Toughness** (Rank 5): +25 HP per rank
- **Shield Block** (Rank 3): 10% chance to block attacks (per rank)
- **Challenging Shout**: Force enemies to attack you
- **Shield Wall** (Ultimate): +50 Defense, reflect 20% damage taken

**Best For**: Tanking, team play, survival

---

**Key Talents**:
- **Flame Touched** (Rank 5): +2 Spell Damage per rank

- **Blizzard** (Ultimate): AoE frost damage and control

**Best For**: Battlefield control, defensive mage

## How to Use Talents

### Accessing the Talent Screen

1. **From Profile**: Click "Talents" button
2. **From Navigation**: Select "Talents" from menu
3. **Hotkey**: Press `T` (if enabled)

### Learning Talents

1. **Select a Tree**: Click on one of the 3 tree tabs
2. **Choose a Talent**: Click on an available talent node (green glow)
3. **Confirm**: Talent is learned immediately
4. **Max Ranks**: Some talents have multiple ranks (1-5)

### Unlearning Talents

- **Right-click** on a learned talent to remove one rank
- Cannot unlearn if other talents depend on it
- Points are refunded immediately

### Respec (Reset All Talents)

- Click **"Reset Talents"** button at bottom
- **Cost**: 50 gold √ó total talent points spent
- **Example**: 9 points = 450 gold
- All points are refunded

## Talent Effects

### Stat Modifiers
Applied directly to your character:

```
+Strength      ‚Üí More damage
+Health        ‚Üí Higher HP pool
+Defense       ‚Üí Damage reduction
+Crit Chance   ‚Üí More critical hits
+Crit Damage   ‚Üí Bigger crits
+Mana Regen    ‚Üí Faster mana recovery
+Movement      ‚Üí Bonus movement range
```

### Passive Abilities

#### **Execute**
- Triggers automatically when enemy HP < 20%
- Bonus damage applies to all attacks
- Great for finishing moves

#### **Bleed**
- Chance to proc on each attack
- Damage over time effect
- Stacks with other bleed effects

#### **Block**
- Automatic chance to block incoming damage
- Reduces damage by 50%
- Increases with ranks

#### **Ignite**
- Procs on critical strikes
- Fire damage over time
- Can spread with Combustion

#### **Lifesteal**
- Heal for % of damage dealt
- Only on critical strikes
- Sustain in long fights

#### **Slow/Freeze**
- Reduces enemy movement
- Freeze prevents movement entirely
- Great for kiting

## Talent Strategy

### Early Game (Levels 2-5)
**Focus**: Foundation talents (Rank 5)
- Max out base stat talents first
- Example: Weapon Mastery 5/5 = +10 STR

### Mid Game (Levels 6-12)
**Focus**: Secondary talents and first capstone
- Branch into specialization
- Unlock Row 2 talents (requires 10 points)

### Late Game (Levels 13-20)
**Focus**: Ultimate talents and optimization
- Get capstone abilities
- Fine-tune build with remaining points

## Tips & Tricks

### ‚úÖ **DO**
- **Plan ahead**: Look at full tree before spending points
- **Max foundation talents first**: Rank 5 talents are most efficient
- **Specialize**: Focus on one tree initially
- **Experiment**: Try different builds for different content
- **Respec for hard content**: Optimize for specific fights

### ‚ùå **DON'T**
- **Spread too thin**: Don't put 1-2 points in every talent
- **Ignore prerequisites**: Plan path to capstone talents
- **Forget to respec**: Don't stick with bad build if not working
- **Skip foundation talents**: They're the most cost-effective
- **Learn without reading**: Always check talent effects first

## Advanced Mechanics

### Talent Scaling
- Stat bonuses scale with ranks (linear)
- Passive effects don't scale (binary on/off)
- Some passives have per-rank scaling (check description)

### Talent Synergies
Certain talent combinations are especially powerful:

**Execute + Precise Strikes + Bladestorm**
- High crit chance
- Massive crit damage
- Execute for finishing blow
- Result: One-shot potential

**Ignite + Combustion + Critical Mass**
- High crit to trigger ignite
- Combustion makes it explode
- Result: Massive AoE burst

**Shield Block + Shield Wall + Toughness**
- High HP pool
- 30% block chance
- Damage reflection
- Result: Unkillable tank

## FAQ

**Q: How many talent points do I get?**
A: 1 point per level, starting at level 2. Max level (20) = 19 points.

**Q: Can I reset my talents?**
A: Yes! Click "Reset Talents" button. Cost = 50 gold √ó points spent.

**Q: Can I put points in multiple trees?**
A: Absolutely! Mix and match as you like. Most builds use 2-3 trees.

**Q: Do talents work in all game modes?**
A: Yes! Talents apply in Story Mode, Tournaments, Free Play, and Grid Combat.

**Q: Can I unlearn a single talent?**
A: Yes, right-click on the talent. Points are refunded immediately.

**Q: What happens if I level up?**
A: You automatically gain 1 talent point. Spend it whenever you're ready.

**Q: Do talents save?**
A: Yes! Your talent choices are saved with your character.

**Q: Can I have multiple builds?**
A: Not yet, but you can respec anytime to change builds.

**Q: What's the best build?**
A: Depends on your playstyle! Experiment to find what works for you.
